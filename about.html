Ascend Creatives

Creators of quality designs and thinkers of fresh ideas.

Ascend Creatives came to existence in 2025 by Akash, Founder & Graphic Designer. We are based out of Mumbai but not limited by its boundary. We are a creative boutique and excel in Branding, Packaging and Graphic Design. We as a team use our expertise to the best and achieve an aesthetic balance between strategic thinking & visual execution. Our portfolio consists of projects in Brand Identity Designs, Marketing Collaterals and Packaging Design to Digital Communication. The Founder anchors the design process with her knowledge and experience spanning four years and counting. We apply both our minds and heart to our design. We first understand you, your business and the true nature of the design problem. Our design process is bespoke and tailored specifically around each brief, as every brand, project and creative challenge is unique. We deliver designs that not only satisfy our client’s imagination but also meet their expectation.
