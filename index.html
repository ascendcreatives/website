About Us

Ascend Creatives

Creators of quality designs and thinkers of fresh ideas.

Ascend Creatives came to existence in 2025 by Aakash Naduvinkeri, Founder & Graphic Designer. We are based out of Mumbai and Goa but not limited by its boundary. We are a creative boutique and excel in Branding, Packaging and Graphic Design. We as a team use our expertise to the best and achieve an aesthetic balance between strategic thinking & visual execution. Our portfolio consists of projects in Brand Identity Designs, Marketing Collaterals and Packaging Design to Digital Communication. The Founder anchors the design process with her knowledge and experience spanning four years and counting. We apply both our minds and heart to our design. We first understand you, your business and the true nature of the design problem. Our design process is bespoke and tailored specifically around each brief, as every brand, project and creative challenge is unique. We deliver designs that not only satisfy our client’s imagination but also meet their expectation.

What We Do

We are a creative design agency that puts our mind to understand your business and our heart to passionately design your visual identity. We work across all aspects of branding, packaging and web design, from ideation to final development.

Branding

Your brand is the single most important investment that you make in the business! When we undertake a Branding project, we spend time to understand your business first and then use our creativity to shape a visual identity that you can be proud of. Your brand’s visual identity is who you are, what you’re worth and what you do. We help you craft the kind of branding which not only attracts your consumers compeling a purchase but also gets your loyal fans!

Print

Connect with your audience through beautifully crafted print media that deliver clear messages. From initial brief right through to artwork creation, we go the extra mile to liaise with your printer and ensure that your results are beyond perfect.

Website

In the digital world, your goal is achieved if you can draw the client’s attention. Our website design is an interesting window into your product or service. We ensure it is creative and intuitive for potential users. The key to achieve this is by flexible templates, dynamic designs, e-commerce opportunities, and SEO optimization. We revamp and offer a new identity to your online presence.

Illustration

From book illustration to mascot designs, along with everything in between, we aspire to give life to your vision. Our Illustrative style is dynamic and lends well to a variety of different applications.
